Hot Dog Buns

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (120ml) warm milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) warm water
  • 2.5 t (9g) active dry yeast
  • 3.5 cups (525g) bread flour
  • 1 T (15g) granulated sugar
  • 1.5 t (7g) fine sea salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2.5 T (35g) softened unsalted butter

Instructions

    1. Combine the milk water and yeast
    2. combine the dry ingredients(flour sugar and salt)
    3. In a stand mixer with the dough hook combine the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients(milk water and yeast mixture) and add one egg yolk, then mix until combined.
    4. add butter and mix once again
    5. shape dough and place in a greased bowl with greased plastic wrap. Let rise until doubled in size
    6. pop and then divide your dough into 12 evenly sized pieces. Shape into a ball, cover and let sit for 10 minutes
    7. shape into a rectangle about 6 inches long and 1-2 inches wide. Place on baking sheet and cover with another baking sheet, let proof for 45 minutes at room temperature
    8. egg wash with one beaten egg and a T of water.
    9. bake at 375Â°F for 15-20 minutes
    10. Generously glaze the buns with melten butter
